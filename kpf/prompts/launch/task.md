Create all launch marketing assets for this knowledge product.

Schema to output:
{schema_json}

Requirements:

sales_page_md:
- Start with the buyer's problem in their words (use pain map language)
- Promise the specific outcome (use the core_promise from product brief)
- List what's included (from deliverables)
- Address 2-3 main objections inline
- End with price and call to action
- Length: 400-600 words

gumroad_listing_md:
- First sentence = the outcome, not the description
- Bullet list of what's included
- Who this is for (1-2 sentences)
- Price justification (what's the alternative cost?)
- Length: 150-250 words

lead_magnet_concept:
- A free resource that demonstrates your expertise in this niche
- Must attract the exact buyer of the paid product
- Must be achievable in 1-2 hours to create
- Format: "The [specific thing] for [specific person]"

launch_posts: 3-5 posts for social media
- Post 1: Problem post (share the pain without selling)
- Post 2: Insight post (teach one thing from the product)
- Post 3: Social proof or story post
- Post 4-5 (optional): Direct offer posts
- Each post 100-250 words, platform-agnostic

faq_md:
- 5-7 questions actual buyers would ask
- Use the objections from the competitor analysis and pain map
- Answers should be honest and specific

objections_md:
- 5 common objections with specific responses
- Format: **Objection:** ... **Response:** ...
- Be honest about limitations while redirecting to value
