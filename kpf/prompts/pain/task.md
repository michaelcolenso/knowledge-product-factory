Map the pain landscape for this niche based on the spending signals provided.

Schema to output:
{schema_json}

Requirements:
- core_question: The single most important recurring unanswered question. Must be specific, not "how do I do X" but the exact phrasing people use.
- patterns: At least 3 distinct pain patterns. Use verbatim-style quotes.
- current_workarounds: What do people actually do today? (hire consultants, use Google, ask colleagues, use outdated PDFs)
- time_wasted: Quantify. "3-6 hours per credentialing attempt" not "a lot of time"
- money_risked: Quantify. "$5,000 in delayed revenue per month" not "significant costs"
- emotional_state: Be specific. "anxious about compliance violations" not "stressed"
- ideal_solution_language: Use their words. What do they say they want?

Pain types:
- information_gap: The answer doesn't exist or is hard to find
- frustration: Existing solutions are bad
- competitor_failure: Paid for something that didn't work
- time_loss: Process is unnecessarily slow
- money_risk: Wrong answer has financial consequences
