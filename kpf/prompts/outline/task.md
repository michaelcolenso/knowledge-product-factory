Create a detailed outline for this knowledge product based on the product brief.

Schema to output:
{schema_json}

Requirements:
- product_title: Final, buyer-facing title (compelling, specific)
- sections: 5-10 sections that cover the complete problem-to-solution journey
- Each section must have:
  - title: Clear, action-oriented section title
  - goal: What the reader achieves by completing this section
  - bullets: 3-6 specific points to cover (not generic, not vague)
  - artifacts_referenced: Which support artifacts (templates, checklists) belong here

Section design principles:
- Start with the problem/context
- Move through the process step-by-step
- End with implementation and maintenance
- Keep scope realistic for 2-4 weeks of creation
- Bullets should be specific enough that a writer knows exactly what to write

Example strong bullets:
- "The 3 CAQH fields that cause the most rejections and how to fill them correctly"
- "When to follow up: the exact timeline that avoids 'lost application' syndrome"

Example weak bullets (avoid):
- "Overview of the process"
- "Tips and tricks"
