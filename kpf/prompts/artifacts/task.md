Design the support artifact manifest for this knowledge product. Identify 3-5 artifacts that will meaningfully increase the product's value.

Schema to output:
{schema_json}

Requirements:
- At least 3 artifacts
- Each artifact must be different from the main product content
- Formats to use: md (markdown), csv (spreadsheet data), txt (plain text templates)
- Each artifact must be immediately usable without reading the main product
- file_name: Use snake_case, no spaces (e.g., "insurance_panel_comparison.csv")

Strong artifact ideas:
- Checklists (what to do in order)
- Templates (fill-in-the-blank documents)
- Calculators/spreadsheets (CSV format with formulas described)
- Email/script templates (copy-paste ready)
- Reference sheets (quick lookup tables)
- Decision trees (if X then Y)

Weak artifacts (avoid):
- Summaries of the main content
- "Additional reading" lists
- Generic templates not tailored to this niche
