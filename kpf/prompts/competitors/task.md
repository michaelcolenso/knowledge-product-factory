{schema_json}

Requirements:
- List at least 3 alternatives (free resources count)
- category must be: "free", "course", "consultant", "software", or "community"
- price: Be specific. "$0", "$47/month", "$1,500 one-time", "$300/hour"
- weaknesses: At least 2 specific reasons this alternative fails. Not "it's expensive" but "it's a 40-hour course when you need a 2-page checklist"
- market_gap_summary: What does NO existing option provide? Be specific.
- switching_reason: The single biggest reason a buyer would choose a new solution over the best existing one

Strong weakness examples:
- "Generic advice not specific to therapists in private practice"
- "Assumes you already know which insurance panels to join"
- "Last updated in 2019, pre-CAQH changes"
- "Requires 40 hours to complete for a 2-page process"
